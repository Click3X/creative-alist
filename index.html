Our People

Company Principals/ Major Executives

Peter Corbett, Founder and President

Having enjoyed a remarkable career as an advertising agency creative and commercial director, Peter Corbett founded Click 3X in 1993. In 1995, Peter founded Media Circus (later renamed IllusionFusion!) one of the first interactive companies. 1997 brought yet another innovative company to fruition when Peter co-founded Sound Lounge, a company that has since become one of the pre-eminent audio post production companies in the country. Peter's awards include several Cannes Lions, Clios and The Crystal Apple Award from the City of New York for his contributions to the film industry.

Jason Mayo, Partner and Managing Director

Jason has played a key role in the strong growth Click 3X has experienced since his arrival in 2001. His responsibilities include, overseeing production as well as managing client relations, talent recruitment and planning for the company's long-term growth. Prior to joining Click 3X, Jason served as executive producer of arc light editorial taking it from start up to a position as a premiere creative editing boutique. Jason has devoted the majority of his career to graphics and visual effects. Jason has served on many trade association boards, he is an active member of BDA / Promax and he writes the popular Dad blog, Out-Numbered.

Directors

Juan Delcan, Director, Designer, Animator

Delcan is the prolific and versatile director of numerous live-action and animated projects, often combining both worlds to create stunning VFX. His varied work includes TV ads, the creation of IDs and broadcast pieces for TV channels in Europe and the United States, short film and documentary.

Jonathan Yi, Director / DP

Jon just wrapped a campaign for Digitas and American Express. This one was similar to other projects he’s done, where in he captured doc footage of small business owners, showing them in their store environments working with customers. Outside of the ad space, Yi has directed three installments of HBO’s award-winning series East of Main Street and shot projects for Twisted Sister, Poison, Daniel Johnston, Mates of State and The Locust. Shift, a film that Jon shot, directed and produced, has also won many accolades and played in many film festivals.

Mark Szumski, Director, SR VFX Artist

With 10 years of experience, Mark has worked on films, commercials and music videos. During his time at Click 3X, he has received five BDA awards, a MTV2 Video of the Year Award and worked on the Venice Film Festival Winner, Man from Plains directed by Jonathan Demme. His animated short, Sleepwalk, has been making news at film festivals across the country this year. He is presently working on yet another new campaign for GEICO, involving the beloved, Maxwell the Pig.

Vellas, Director

Brazilian director Vellas works in tandem with Sentimental Filme, the director's Sao Paulo-based production company. His spot for Leica, Alma (Soul), earned five Lions at this year's Festival of Creativity in Cannes and was also shown in the New Directors' Showcase. It won Gold for Cinematography, Silver for Direction, two Bronze awards for Art Direction and Editing, and one Silver in the Film category for Retail Stores. Vellas (born Felipe Vellasco) began his career as an art director for agencies such as DM9DDB and Ogilvy & Mather before becoming an art director and animator for production companies.

Daniel Powell, Director

With a background in comedy, Dan brings a distinct perspective to Click3X. Aside from his duties at Click 3X and 'Inside Amy Schumer' on Comedy Central, recently renewed for a second season, he also produced the show ‘Ugly Americans’, which ran on Comedy Central from 2010-2012, and the MTV series 'Hey Girl' which he co-developed and directed the pilot episode. He served as executive in charge of production on the hit first season of ‘Important Things with Demetri Martin’, as well as the Emmy and Peabody award-winning ‘Colbert Report’. Prior to being a network executive, he was an associate producer on ‘The Daily Show with Jon Stewart’ and a contributing writer to Robert Smigel’s 'TV Funhouse' segments on 'Saturday Night Live.'

2013 Highlights

BBDO challenged Click3X and ClickFire Media to create the Elements of Everyday Life, a Vine-based video content site for the Samsung Galaxy Active. Using a roster writer director team, Click3X shot more than 30 Vines. ClickFire Media then produced a responsive design website based on the content, fully integrating with Vine and the Twitter Card API for enhanced social-share visibility.




Created for the Promax BDA conference, ‘Sleepwalk’ is the creation of Click3x, Director / CD, Mark Szumski. A cel animated music video for a classic tune, harkening back to a time before music videos were ever used to promote songs. ‘Sleepwalk’ has been recognized by several animation film festivals and was the employee pick by Vimeo to open their site for an entire day.




Major Campaigns / Spots

Click 3X has been working with The Martin Agency on the GEICO campaign, specifically on all spots with Maxwell The Pig, for almost 3 years now. The newest campaign consists of 7 spots that will start rolling out on Black Friday.




The Estee Lauder Companies’ hired our beauty and fashion group, Raison D'Être to create the 2013 Breast Cancer Awareness Campaign. It’s built as a global social experience that encourages participants from all around the world to create their own “Circles of Strength” comprised of friends, family, co-workers and loved ones. The 2 minute “Circles of Strength” video was directed by our own, Juan Delcan.




Armor All - A shareable video experience. ClickFire Media was challenged by Source Marketing to create a customized sharable video experience for their client, Armor All. The core of the project uses Google Maps and Google Map's Street View API in conjunction with a live action video. This project was part of a larger campaign featuring Armor All's updated Viking mascot.
